About Me

Adriana Serna is a native of California's Bay Area and a dedicated academic art student. She is studying at San Jose State University, with a goal in earning her Bachelors in Fine Arts, emphasizing in teaching. She is a strong believer that art lives in everyone and everything; being able to teach her community is just one way to help people connect with art that surrounds them. Art is her passion which inspires her to inspire others.
